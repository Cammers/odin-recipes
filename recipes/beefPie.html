Beef Pie

Learn to crate our lovely beef pie

Created using fresh cuts of beef within a lovely sauce

Ingrediants

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Pastry
  • Egg
  • Red wine

Steps to create

  1. Braise the beef within a pan
  2. add stock to the braised beef
  3. add the red wine to the stock
  4. Cover with a lid and cook for 50 minutes
  5. add filling to dish
  6. cover the dish with the puff pastry
  7. bake until golden
  8. serve at the table
