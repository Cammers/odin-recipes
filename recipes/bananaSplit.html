Lasagna

Make our most popular lasagna today to find out what all the fuss is about!

Made up of multiple layers of ragu as well as creamy white sauce, and topped with a layer of cheese.

Ingrediants

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps to create

  1. Make the sauce
  2. cook the sheets
  3. Make the white sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let it rest
  6. Serve at the table with a side salad
