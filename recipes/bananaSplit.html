Banana Split

Create a lovely pudding to acompany any dish

Made up of simple ingrediants that can be customised to your liking

Ingrediants

  • Banana
  • Cream
  • Chocoalte
  • Sprinkles
  • ice cream

Steps to create

  1. cut the banana in half and add to a dish
  2. scoop on your desired amount of ice cream
  3. customise your banana split with what ever toppings you want
  4. dig in at the table!
